Who We Are

An innovative and agile real estate firm that can think outside the box

Diversified Experience

Lindenmuth Realty is a diversified real estate management, investment, and brokerage firm, with over 15 years of extensive expertise in residential, commercial, retail, and office real estate.

The company currently owns and manages over 100 residential properties, 90,000 square feet of office space, and 100,000 square feet of retail space throughout Texas and Michigan.

We have rehabbed and sold over 120 houses primarily acquired at tax and mortgage foreclosure auctions and extended over 150 hard money loans to a wide variety of clients.

Lindenmuth Realty and its principal are always looking for great investment opportunities and has the resources and ability to act quickly, with partners who also have extensive expertise in all facets of real estate.

Lindenmuth Realty is a licensed brokerage in Texas and Michigan.

Justin Lindenmuth

Broker/Owner

Justin Lindenmuth, principal and broker/owner of Lindenmuth Realty, knew early in life he wanted to be involved in some sort of business. Growing up in Austin, TX, he spent time honing his business acumen by running an eBay store buying and selling baseball cards. While majoring in Business and Economics at Southwestern University in Georgetown, TX, his father recommended taking a look at real estate as a career path. He obtained his real estate license in 2003 and, shortly after graduating in 2005, his broker’s license.

Out of college, Justin’s first job was for a startup homebuilding company. There he learned the ins and outs of running a high-growth business along with the logistics and expertise of developing plats of land and building houses. Seeking more opportunity, Justin left the company in early 2008 to start buying houses at foreclosure and flipping them. Cutting his teeth on his own during a severe economic downturn set the tone for the rest of his career.

Since 2008, Justin has been involved in many facets of real estate in both residential and commercial: buying, selling, management, leasing (landlord and tenant representation), development, rehabilitation, and construction.
